# Returns & Refunds

## Drinks and food
If you are unhappy with a drink or food item, tell any staff member within 15 minutes of receiving it and we will remake it or refund it, no questions asked. We cannot refund an item that has been mostly consumed unless there was a genuine problem with it.

## Retail bags of beans
- Unopened bags of beans can be returned within 30 days with your receipt for a full refund.
- Opened bags can be exchanged (not refunded) within 14 days if you did not enjoy the roast — we would rather find you a coffee you love than have you stuck with one you don't. This exchange is limited to one bag per customer per month.
- Grinder and brewing equipment carry the manufacturer's warranty; bring the receipt and we will help you file a claim.

## Gift cards
Gift cards are non-refundable and do not expire. Lost gift cards can be replaced only if you registered the card at the counter when you bought it.

## How refunds are issued
Refunds go back to the original payment method within 3 to 5 business days. Cash purchases are refunded in cash on the spot.
