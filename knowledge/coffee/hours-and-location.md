# Hours & Location — Brew & Bindery Coffee

## Opening hours
- Monday to Friday: 6:30 AM to 7:00 PM
- Saturday: 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM
- Sunday: 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM
- We are closed on Thanksgiving, Christmas Day, and New Year's Day.

The kitchen stops taking hot-food orders 30 minutes before closing, but drinks and pastries are served right up to close.

## Location & parking
We are at 214 Maple Street, in the old bindery building on the corner of Maple and 3rd. Street parking is free after 6 PM and all day Sunday. There is a paid lot behind the building (enter from 3rd Street) that validates for two hours with any purchase over $8.

## Contact
Phone: (555) 0142. We answer the phone during opening hours only. For catering and large orders, email orders@brewandbindery.example and allow one business day for a reply.
