# Membership & Catering

## The Bindery Club (membership)
Membership costs $12 per month and includes:
- One free drip coffee or tea every day
- 15% off all retail bags of beans and equipment
- Early access to limited single-origin roasts before they go on sale to everyone
- A free pastry on your birthday

You can pause your membership for up to three months per year at no cost, and cancel anytime — there is no cancellation fee and no minimum term. Membership perks cannot be combined with other discounts on the same item.

## Wi-Fi and workspace
Wi-Fi is free; the network is "BinderyGuest" and the password is printed on your receipt. During weekday mornings before 11 AM we ask that laptops be limited to the back room so the front counter stays quick for the morning rush.

## Catering
We cater meetings and events. Options include boxed pastries, coffee travelers (each serves about 12 cups), and staffed espresso carts for larger events. We need 48 hours notice for boxes and travelers, and one week for a staffed cart. A 50% deposit holds the date; the balance is due on delivery. Delivery is free within 5 miles for orders over $75.
